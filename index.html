A King Was Born

King James was born Lebron Raymone James on December 30 1984. Lebron at the early age of 11 started playing organized basketball and continued playing with local friends up through high school where he attended St. Mary High School. Lebron throughout his high school career was an amazing athlete averaging almost 32 points and 10 rebounds a game his senior year. By that time he was being regarded as the King James who would soon take the NBA by storm.
